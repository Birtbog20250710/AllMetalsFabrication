Frequently Asked Questions

What is your average lead time for high-volume orders?

Our standard lead time is 4-6 weeks for initial production runs, with expedited options available. Repeat orders typically ship in 2-3 weeks thanks to our lean manufacturing processes and dedicated production cells for high-volume partners.

Do you offer design for manufacturability (DFM) support?

Yes, our engineering team provides complimentary DFM analysis for all high-volume projects. We'll identify cost reduction opportunities and suggest design optimizations that maintain quality while improving manufacturability.

What materials can you work with?

We specialize in aluminum, stainless steel, carbon steel, and various alloys. Our expertise includes both standard and exotic materials required for automotive and medical applications, with full material certification and traceability.

How do you ensure quality for critical components?

Our ISO 9001:2015 certified quality system includes first article inspection, in-process verification, and final inspection with CMM validation. We maintain full documentation and provide PPAP packages for automotive customers.
