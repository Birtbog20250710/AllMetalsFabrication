FAQ

What part sizes are ideal?
Most small-to-mid sized weldments that can be hand-guided and fixtured quickly. Share your print and we’ll confirm fit and fixturing approach.
How do you handle high-mix changeovers?
Cobot cells with teach-by-hand programming (~10 minutes typical) plus modular fixtures—so swapping SKUs is fast without sacrificing quality.
Do you certify welds?
Yes. AMF operates under an ISO 9001:2015 quality management system with documented WPS/WPQR as required by the project.
Lead times?
Quotes in 1–3 days. Pilot runs scheduled quickly; production releases based on material/fixture readiness.
